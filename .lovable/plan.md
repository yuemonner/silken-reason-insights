

# Silken Reason — Personal Digital Magazine

## Overview
A minimalist, magazine-style personal website for **Yue H.**, Founder & Principal Analyst of **Silken Reason**. The site blends old-money elegance with a modern geek aesthetic through glass-morphism effects, gradient overlays, and a refined anthracite + champagne gold color palette.

---

## Design System

- **Background**: Anthracite gray (#28282B) — textured, not flat black
- **Text**: Off-white (#F5F5F5)
- **Accent**: Champagne Gold (#F7E7CE) — used on buttons, links, logo, hover states
- **Glass effect**: Semi-transparent cards with backdrop blur and subtle borders
- **Gradients**: Soft gold-to-transparent gradients on section transitions and hero elements
- **Headings font**: Playfair Display (serif — classic, old-money elegance)
- **Body font**: Roboto Mono (monospace — geek, modern, code-like)

---

## Navigation
A sleek **horizontal top navbar** with:
- **Silken Reason** logo/wordmark on the left (champagne gold)
- Section links: THE LOGIC · THE SILK · THE ETHER · ADVISORY
- Minimalist, no clutter — text links with gold underline on hover
- Mobile-responsive hamburger menu

## Content System
- Articles stored as **markdown files** in the codebase
- Each article has frontmatter (title, date, section, tags, cover image)
- Sample articles pre-loaded for each section to demonstrate the layout
- Easy to add new articles by dropping in new `.md` files
